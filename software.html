Software

We develop and maintain open research software for statistical genetics, polygenic prediction, multi-omics integration, pleiotropy analysis, mediation analysis, and transcriptome-wide association studies.

Source code is available through the biostatpzeng GitHub account.

DPR

Latent Dirichlet process regression

A non-parametric genetic prediction framework for complex traits, designed to model latent genetic architecture and improve risk prediction from genome-wide markers.

transPGS

Transfer-learning polygenic score

Transfer-learning methods for polygenic score construction, with emphasis on improving genetic prediction in underrepresented populations by leveraging cross-population information.

TLegene

Transfer-learning eGene analysis

A transfer-learning framework for eGene identification that borrows genetic similarity across samples to strengthen expression quantitative trait analysis.

MAIUP

Mixture-adjusted pleiotropy testing

Mixture-adjusted intersection-union testing for detecting pleiotropic associations under composite null hypotheses in genetic and omics studies.

HMAT

Aggregated transcriptome-wide association

Harmonic mean p-value aggregation for transcriptome-wide association studies, combining evidence across expression prediction models.

MTV

TWAS-mediated variance estimation

A method for transcriptome-wide association analysis and mediated variance estimation, supporting the study of gene expression-mediated disease mechanisms.

IUSMMT

Survival mediation analysis

Intersection-union survival mixture-adjusted mediation testing for high-dimensional omics data and time-to-event outcomes.

iMAP

Integrative pleiotropic mapping

An integrative mapping approach for pleiotropic association and annotation selection in genome-wide association studies.
